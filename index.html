About Ataraxia

Ataraxia is a word that means inner peace — a quiet steadiness of the mind.

This café was created as a soft escape from the rush of everyday life, inspired by warm wooden interiors, gentle lighting, and the comforting atmosphere found in Studio Ghibli–like worlds.

Every cake is made to feel familiar, every cup of coffee brewed with care. Whether you come alone with a book or share laughter with friends, Ataraxia is a place where time slows down, and peace quietly settles in.
